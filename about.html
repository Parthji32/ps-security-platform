Why Deception?

Traditional security tools are reactive: detect attacks after they've already breached perimeter defenses. Deception is proactive: place fake assets where attackers look, and catch them before they reach anything real.

The math is simple: any interaction with a deception asset is malicious by definition. This means zero false positives, high-confidence alerts, and faster response.
