FAQ

What is deception technology? ▼

Deception technology uses fake assets (honeypots, canary tokens, decoys) to detect attackers. Any interaction with a deception asset is malicious by definition, resulting in zero false positives.

How is Kavach v1 different from canarytokens.org? ▼

Canarytokens.org is free but basic. Kavach v1 adds real-time attacker fingerprinting, team management, integrations (Slack/Datadog), correlation analysis, and 90-day log retention.

Is there a free tier? ▼

Yes! Kavach v1 Solo tier is free: 10 canary tokens, basic fingerprinting, email alerts, 7-day logs. Upgrade to Team ($299/mo) for unlimited tokens and advanced features.

Can I self-host Kavach? ▼

Kavach v2 ships as Docker Compose, so yes — deploy on your own servers, VPC, or air-gapped networks. Kavach v1 is SaaS-only, but enterprise self-hosting is available (custom pricing).

Do you store my application data? ▼

No. We only store token metadata, attack events, and your account info. We never see or store your real application code, data, or users. You own 100% of your data.

What compliance certifications do you have? ▼

We're working toward SOC 2 Type II and ISO 27001 (Q4 2026). GDPR compliant now. HIPAA BAA available on request. NIS2 support coming for Kavach v2.

How do I get support? ▼

Solo: Community support. Team: Priority email support with SLA. Enterprise: 24/7 dedicated Slack support. Email support@ps-security.io anytime.
